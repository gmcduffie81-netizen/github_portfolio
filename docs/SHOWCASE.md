## The problem I got tired of

Field companies run on shared passwords, spreadsheets nobody trusts, “call so-and-so” for every claim, techs guessing on van paperwork, and bosses flying blind until Friday.

I built the opposite: **tools people open every morning** — and I still handle the messy human parts myself.

| Name | Who uses it | What they get |

| **Ops Console** | Managers, fleet, claims | Claims, vans, people, reports, access switches |
